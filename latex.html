My thoughts on latex

This is a wonderful typesetting system I ever came across for writing mathematical content. It is just impossible to write mathematics without latex. Every time I use latex I thank Donald Knuth a million times and I use it every day almost surely. So I thank Knuth everyday with probability 1. Latex is generally pronounced as lay-tek [as in technology]. Latex is amazing for making presentations also.
